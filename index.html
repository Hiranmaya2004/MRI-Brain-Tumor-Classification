Core Findings

Saliency Stability Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of Grad-CAM explanation robustness under three types of structured perturbations across all 1,600 test images.

⚠️

Key Finding

Prediction stability does not guarantee explanation stability. The model retains correct predictions under all perturbations, yet saliency maps undergo significant spatial drift — with Pearson correlation dropping as low as 0.15 under FGSM adversarial attack and IoU dropping to 0.32 at ε=0.05.

Gaussian Noise

x̃ = x + η, η ~ N(0, σ²I)

Additive white Gaussian noise at σ ∈ {0.01, 0.05, 0.10}

Rician Noise

x̃ = √((x+η₁)² + η₂²)

MRI-realistic noise from magnitude reconstruction

FGSM Attack

x̃ = x + ε·sign(∇ₓL)

Adversarial perturbation at ε ∈ {0.001, 0.005, 0.01, 0.02, 0.05}
